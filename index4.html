A b o u t A l b i o n

Albion Online is a sandbox MMORPG set in an open medieval fantasy world. The game has a player-driven economy, where almost every item is created by someone. You can combine various elements of armor and weapons due to the absence of strict binding to the class system, because you are what you wear. Explore the world, participate in exciting battles with other adventurers, capture territories and build your own house.

From basic tools and clothing to impressive armor and powerful weapons, almost every item in the game is created by players in buildings built by players, from resources collected again by players. Buy, sell and trade with other players in local markets around the world of Albion. Create rare and powerful items, then sell them to those who will offer a high price, and multiply your wealth.
